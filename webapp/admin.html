Seat-filler Bots

Bots help a lobby reach the minimum player count faster when real players are waiting. They're always labeled "🤖" everywhere, they never cost a real player anything, and they can never win or take a share of the prize pool — that stays 100% reserved for real players. You control whether bots are on and how many join each lobby; there's no odds/probability dial because that would mean the house deciding who wins, which we don't do.

Bot Names

Only the display name shown to players is editable here — one bot is randomly given one of these names (plus the 🤖 label) each time it seat-fills a lobby. There is intentionally no control over how often a bot "wins", because bots never win real money games (see above).
